Liquidity Management

To prevent excessive liquidity accumulation and ensure token price growth, MoonBurgerCrypto has implemented a Liquidity Cap Mechanism:

  • Triggered manually by the team for fine-tuning.
  • Capped at 10% of total supply (if exceeded, action is taken).
  • Gradual removal of 10% of excess liquidity when triggered.
  • Allocation of removed liquidity:
    • 50% to Marketing & Development
    • 50% to Reserve Fund

This prevents excessive stability while allowing price movements based on real market demand.

Passive Income Opportunities

MoonBurgerCrypto offers multiple ways for holders to earn passive income:

  • 1. Staking Rewards: Holders can stake MBGR tokens to earn rewards distributed every 7 days, with compound interest applied.
  • 2. Yield Farming: Users can provide liquidity to MoonBurgerCrypto pairs and earn rewards based on transaction fees and farming incentives.
  • 3. Reflection Rewards: A percentage of every transaction is redistributed to existing holders, incentivizing long-term holding.
  • 4. NFT Staking & Rewards (Upcoming Feature): MoonBurgerCrypto plans to integrate NFT staking, allowing users to stake NFTs in return for exclusive rewards and passive income boosts.
  • 5. Liquidity Pool Rewards: By contributing to MoonBurgerCrypto’s liquidity pool, users earn a share of trading fees, ensuring a stable price floor while generating additional income.
  • 6. Auto-Compounding Interest: MoonBurgerCrypto’s staking system automatically compounds earnings, maximizing returns over time. Users can reinvest or withdraw their earnings as desired.

Buyback & Burn Strategy

MoonBurgerCrypto incorporates a team-controlled buyback and burn mechanism to maintain price appreciation and scarcity:

  • Buybacks are executed manually by the team based on market conditions.
  • All purchased tokens are permanently burned, reducing supply over time.
  • Burns do not interfere with passive income payouts.

This ensures token deflation while maintaining flexibility in market strategy.
