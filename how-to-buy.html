🚀 How to Buy MoonBurger (MBGR)

Follow these simple steps to get your hands on $MBGR and join the MoonBurger community!

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Get a Binance Smart Chain-compatible wallet:
    • 🦊 MetaMask
    • 🔵 Trust Wallet
    • 🛡 SafePal
  2. Buy BNB (Binance Coin) from an exchange:
    • Binance (Fastest way)
    • Coinbase
    • Crypto.com
  3. Swap BNB for $MBGR on PancakeSwap:
    • Open PancakeSwap and click “Connect Wallet.”
    • Paste the MoonBurger contract address: [YOUR TOKEN CONTRACT ADDRESS]
    • Swap BNB for $MBGR and confirm the transaction.
  4. Add $MBGR to your wallet manually:
    • Open your wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or SafePal).
    • Click “Add Custom Token” and paste the contract address.
    • Your $MBGR tokens should now be visible in your wallet!

🤔 FAQ

Why do I need BNB?

BNB is required to pay transaction fees on the Binance Smart Chain.

What if my transaction fails?

Try increasing the slippage tolerance to 5-10% in PancakeSwap settings.

Where can I see my $MBGR tokens?

Add the contract address manually in your wallet under “Add Custom Token.”
