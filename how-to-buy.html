🚀 How to Buy MoonBurger ($MBGR)

Follow these simple steps to swap BNB for $MBGR on PancakeSwap!

📌 Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

You need a crypto wallet that supports Binance Smart Chain (BSC).

  • 🔹 MetaMask (Browser Extension & Mobile)
  • 🔹 Trust Wallet (Mobile App)

Once installed, make sure to enable **Binance Smart Chain (BSC)** in the settings.

📌 Step 2: Buy BNB

BNB is needed to swap for $MBGR. You can buy it from:

  • 🔹 Binance
  • 🔹 Crypto.com

After buying, withdraw BNB to your wallet using the **BEP20 (BSC) network**.

📌 Step 3: Swap BNB for $MBGR on PancakeSwap

Now, you're ready to buy MoonBurger!

  • ✅ Open PancakeSwap.
  • ✅ Click **"Connect Wallet"** and select MetaMask or Trust Wallet.
  • ✅ Paste the MoonBurger contract address (TBA) in the token field.
  • ✅ Set slippage to **5-10%** if needed.
  • ✅ Click **Swap**, confirm the transaction in your wallet, and wait for confirmation.

📌 Step 4: Add $MBGR to Your Wallet

If you don’t see $MBGR in your wallet:

  • ✅ Open your wallet and click **"Add Custom Token"**.
  • ✅ Select **Binance Smart Chain (BSC)**.
  • ✅ Paste the MoonBurger contract address (TBA).

🎉 Congrats! You Now Own $MBGR 🍔🚀
