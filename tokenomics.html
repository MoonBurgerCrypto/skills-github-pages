📢 Tokenomics FAQ

🚀 The total supply of **MoonBurger ($MBGR)** is **1,000,000,000 tokens** (1 billion).
💰 Every transaction has a **4.5% fee**, which is split as follows:
  • 🏦 **1.5% Liquidity Fee** (Added to the liquidity pool for stability)
  • 🔥 **0.5% Buy & Burn** (Removes tokens from supply)
  • 🛠️ **1% Development Fund** (Funds new features & improvements)
  • 📢 **1% Marketing & Partnerships**
  • 👨‍💻 **0.5% Team Allocation**
🔥 The **Buy & Burn** mechanism takes a portion of transaction fees to **buy back $MBGR** and **permanently burn it**. This reduces supply, making the token more scarce and potentially more valuable.
🏦 The **Liquidity Fee** adds funds to the liquidity pool on PancakeSwap, ensuring smooth trading and preventing huge price swings.
🛠️ The **Development Fund** supports ongoing improvements, including:
  • New features & upgrades
  • Marketing & partnerships
  • Community events & rewards
🔥 If all tokens were burned (which won’t happen), **no one would have any $MBGR left to trade!** That’s why burning is controlled and gradual, keeping enough tokens in circulation for the project to grow.
📊 You can track $MBGR on:
  • 🔍 **BscScan** (for token contract details)
  • 📈 **CoinGecko & CoinMarketCap** (once listed)
